Announcing RTXI 2015

We're excited to announce RTXI 2015, a conference for us to gather and discuss new ideas in real-time biological research and how RTXI can carry them out. Register here, and we'll see you there!


When
May 7-8, 2015

Where

The Georgia Academy of Medicine

conveniently located close to hotels, public transportation, and the airport.


Why

RTXI 2015 is a small meeting to discuss ongoing efforts in applying real-time methods to experiments in biology and physiology. While this conference is targeting and highlighting users of our RTXI software (http://www.rtxi.org), all are welcome! This meeting builds upon the success of two meetings we hosted approximately a decade ago in Atlanta (GT Real 2005) and Boston (BU Real 2006).

Much has changed in ten years, both in terms of hardware and the increasing number of experimental techniques using real-time methods. This conference features a single track session for two days, along with workshops and a poster session. Ample time in structured and open sessions will give us all a chance to share ideas, software, and technical knowledge.

Events

  • Feature Speakers

We will host talks and poster presentation from attendees wanting to give them. If you'd like to present anything, let us know in the conference registration form. Once the details of this event are finalized, look here for session details.

Our current RTXI developers will hold a workshop to help new users get started with RTXI and demonstrate v2.0 to current ones. Emphasis will be given to module development.
