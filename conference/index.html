Announcing RTXI 2015

We're excited to announce RTXI 2015, a conference for us to gather and discuss new ideas in real-time biological research and how RTXI can carry them out. Register here, and we'll see you there!


When
May 7-8, 2015

Where
The Georgia Academy of Medicine

Why
There have been many new developments and applications of RTXI, making 2015 an excellent time for gathering to discuss the potentials of real-time biological experimentation. This conference will give us all a chance to share ideas, software, and technical knowledge.

Events

  • Featured Speakers
  • Talks/Poster Session
  • RTXI Workshop

We're excited to have the following people come to talk with us about their research and/or real-time applications.

Chuck Dorval, University of Utah

Flavio Frohlich, UNC

Astrid Prinz, Emory University

Dieter Jaeger, Emory University

Scott Stanslanski, Medtronic

We will host talks and poster presentation from attendees wanting to give them. If you'd like to present anything, let us know in the conference registration form. Once the details of this event are finalized, look here for session details.

Our current RTXI developers will hold a workshop to help new users get started with RTXI and demonstrate v2.0 to current ones. Emphasis will be given to module development.

RTXI is funded by NIH grant R01-RR020115.
