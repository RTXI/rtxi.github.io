18 /*
19 * The ZAP current stimulus is described by:
20 * I(t) = Io*sin [2*pi*f(t)*t], with f(t) = fo + (fm - fo) * (t/T)
21 *
22 * Sample parameters:
23 * fo = 0 Hz to fm = 20 Hz (2 Hz/s)
24 * fo = 10 Hz to fm = 40 Hz (3 Hz/s) over a total time period of T=10 s.
25 *
26 * In some cells we also tested the reversed protocol (reverse ZAP) where
27 * frequency was varied from 20 to 0 or 40 to 10 Hz.
28 */

36 GeneratorZap::GeneratorZap()

48  wave.push_back(amplitude * sin(TWOPI * freqtime * i * dt));
